PRODUCER QUESTIONS,
ANSWERED

WHERE IN LAS VEGAS DOES Z SQUARED CREW?

Anywhere in the Las Vegas Valley — the Strip, Downtown, Allegiant Stadium, T-Mobile Arena, Michelob Ultra Arena, the Las Vegas Convention Center, Henderson, Summerlin, and North Las Vegas. We've also crewed regularly at SoFi Stadium and other LA venues. See our Las Vegas page for venue-specific details.

ARE YOU AVAILABLE 24/7?

Yes. Vegas is a 24-hour town and we crew accordingly. After-hours, weekend, and same-day bookings are standard.

HOW FAST CAN YOU TURN A QUOTE?

Same business day. Most quotes go out within 2 hours. Same-day crews available when gear and operators line up.

DO YOU SUB-RENT GEAR OR OWN EVERYTHING?

Almost everything is owned in-house — 2× Sony FX9, Arri Amira, Sony FX3, 2× Sony PMW-350, Sony PXW-500, Canon Cine Servo lenses, Rokinon Cine DS prime kit, Aputure LS 600d/600x, Litepanels Astra (×4), Sound Devices 633, Lectrosonics 411, fiber + TVU. No rental layer added on top of crew rates. See full gear list →

CAN YOU CREW MULTI-DAY, MULTI-CITY TOURS?

Yes — we routinely travel for network shoots. Vegas is the home base; we've crewed regularly in LA, Phoenix, Indianapolis (Combine), and Boston. Travel days bill at industry standard, and we own the kit so there's no rental layer added on top.
